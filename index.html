Our roots

Part of something
much bigger than Milan.

MiYES was founded in 2010 as the youth of the El Shaddai Charismatic Community in Milan. We are part of one of the largest Catholic lay movements in the world, born in the Philippines and now present across the globe.

We are young Filipinos living in Milan, bringing our faith, our culture, and our community with us — and sharing it with whoever wants to be part of it.

Our home is Piazza Santa Maria del Carmine 3, in the heart of Brera, where we gather, pray, and serve together.

El Shaddai DWXI PPFI The movement we belong to
Founded in Manila in 1981 by Bro. Mike Velarde. Now a global Catholic movement present in over 30 countries.
Over 9 million members worldwide. The largest lay Catholic organisation in the world.
Fully approved by and linked to the Catholic Church, with charismatic worship, Scripture, and Eucharist at its heart.
El Shaddai means "a God who is more than enough" — a God present and generous in our everyday lives.
"The movement allows people to really connect to the Word of God."
Our calling

Passing the torch —
one generation at a time.

At the heart of MiYES is a conviction: faith is not something you keep to yourself. It is something you hand on. Every generation that has been part of this community has carried the same responsibility — to seek God sincerely, and to inspire those who come after to do the same.

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."
1 Timothy 4:12

We believe that young people are not the future of the Church. They are the Church, here and now. MiYES exists to be a place where the youngest among us encounter a living God — and where that encounter becomes a mission they carry forward.

"Even youths grow tired and weary, but those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength."
Isaiah 40:30–31
"Remember your Creator in the days of your youth, before the evil days come."
Ecclesiastes 12:1
"How can a young person stay on the path of purity? By living according to your word."
Psalm 119:9
"Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these."
Luke 18:16

The faces in our community change with every year. But the mission does not. Each generation of MiYES is called to be a living witness — to the generation before, and to those still to come.

What we do

Life in the community

Monthly service

Once a month we come together to serve through prayer, practical help, and presence within our community in Milan. Service is not what we do. It is who we are.

El Shaddai Youth Global Network

We actively participate in events and activities organised by the El Shaddai Youth Global Network, connecting with young Catholics from the Philippines and across the world.

Prayer and fellowship

Regular gatherings for worship, Scripture, and fellowship at our home in Piazza Santa Maria del Carmine — the heart of MiYES life in Milan.
